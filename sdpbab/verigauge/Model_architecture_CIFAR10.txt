Models are taken from: Li, et al. SoK: Certified Robustness for Deep Neural Networks, arxiv 2020.

All cifar10 are fully-connected feedforward ReLU networks, where

cifar10_A_certadv_2255: 2 hidden layer, 20 neurons each hidden layer, epsilon=2/255
cifar10_B_certadv_2255: 3 hidden layer, 100 neurons each hidden layer, epsilon=2/255
